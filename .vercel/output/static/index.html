FAQ

Common Questions from School Leaders

Everything you need to know before bringing Deltra to your school.

Deltra is fully compliant with Indonesia's UU PDP (Personal Data Protection Law). All data is encrypted at rest and in transit, hosted on AWS ap-southeast-1 (Jakarta), and never shared with third parties. We are aligned with ISO 27001 standards and SOC 2 Type II certification is in progress.

Most schools are fully live within 3–5 business days. Our onboarding team handles data migration, configures your school's academic calendar and curriculum structure, and runs live training sessions for admin staff and teachers. Zero technical expertise required from your team.

Deltra is designed for non-technical users. Teachers typically need 1–2 hours to get comfortable with the platform. We provide in-app walkthroughs, a dedicated help center in Bahasa Indonesia, and live support via WhatsApp during school hours.

Yes. Deltra has native integrations with DAPODIK and SisPenA, and supports CSV/Excel import for historical grade and attendance data. Custom API integrations are available on the Yayasan plan.

You own your data. Upon request, we export all your school's data in standard formats (CSV, Excel, PDF) at no cost. Data is retained for 90 days after account closure, then permanently deleted from our servers.

Pricing is structured per school, not per student — so you won't face surprise costs as enrollment grows. Each plan includes unlimited students, with tiered pricing based on the number of active staff accounts and feature set. Contact us for a custom quote.
